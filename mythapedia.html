Mythapedia

A structured cosmology and archive for mapping mythology.

Mythapedia is an ongoing project to organise mythological traditions into a coherent, cross-referential system. It brings together narrative, structure, and symbolic correspondence into a unified framework.

Rather than retelling myths, it maps relationships — between figures, functions, and cosmological roles — across cultures and time.

The work is evolving and will expand into a full corpus over time.
